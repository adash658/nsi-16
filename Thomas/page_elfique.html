I Naugrim

        I Naugrim nawath dúnadhad ar gaug, cuinar nu i ered. Nathrar naid-ceneded vi Endor an i maenas beleg hain naid-tengath. Main ennas tírad naeg o fuin en-edraith ar cerith manwaith a maglawath. Noerweg hain, i Naugrim nawath norned tirim, carer i gelydh hain vi i warnath, pediar an tannath i gwystath ve anann gonn. Berioled, ú-thaer ú-geriar taltad mi dagor, dregoed gobelin a orch atha dramm na’n achad vail. I Naugrim pen-nath edregol o lhaw-mith ar melir prestad i dhû vi tavernd bain na mellonath.”

Gimli, Brennil Naug
