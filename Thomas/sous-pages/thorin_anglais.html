Description of Thorin

         Thorin is the son of Thráin and the grandson of Thrór. He took part in the exodus of the dwarves of Erebor toward the Blue Mountains after the attack of Smaug the Terrible, a mighty dragon of Morgoth. After his grandfather was slain by Azog the Pale Orc, Thorin led the assault on Moria, the last stronghold of the orcs, with the help of six armies of dwarves rallied by his father. He distinguished himself in this battle by cutting off an oak branch to use as a shield after his own was shattered. This is how he earned the name “Oakenshield.” Thorin is best known for leading the quest to reclaim Erebor and free it from Smaug the Terrible, together with his company of twelve other dwarves, Gandalf the Grey—one of the five wizards—and Bilbo, a Hobbit.
