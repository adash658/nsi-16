Thorin Doronbreth

Aded

Pethad en-Thorin

        Thorin ná ion Thráin ar enorion Thrór. Gered hain vi i runcad en-i Naugrim o Erebor na i Ered Luin ab i dagad Smaug i Beleg, amlug balch en-Morgoth. An i anorath hain na marn Athzug, i Orchor-fain, Thorin orthed i Moria, i methad-naid en-orcath, as estolad en-eneg hwestaidad Naugrin go estel adar hain. Tangad hain vi i dagor hen, odrogthar rhamen doron an edrosta i thôlath vreged lín. Edain hen gannath “Doronbreth”. Thorin ná linnadh beleg sui i hûd en-edorad an Erebor a i thandad o Smaug i Beleg, as gwanod en dodecaid Naugrin, as Gandalf i Mithren, erin Vîn Istarion, ar as Bilbo, Perian.
