Erebor, Felas Naugrin

Aded

Pethad en-Erebor

        Erebor, egor i Amon Erui, na oron erin 1067 rennin. Dan i hûn nu-doron na-gerith i brand dorthad a mâthad. An sui dregir Moria an-cause balrog erin er-echair, i Naugrim ad-established felas beleg nu i Amon Erui, sâd gwanur an i beria maltha. I Naugrim ennas hantir anann anegath maltha laur ar mithril, a thandir i cothi palan. Gennir i Arkenstone, egor i Sarn Aer, vi ’waun en i oron. I sarn hen, i nephad en-taur Naugrin, dól na-lost vi i nauthad Smaug na-Erebor, dan ad-gennin vi i rîd Thorin.
